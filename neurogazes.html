Normal Gaze

All pathways are intact. Both eyes can move coordinately in all horizontal directions.

Pathway Rule: To look RIGHT, the LEFT FEF initiates. The signal travels to the RIGHT PPRF. The Right PPRF activates Right CN VI (Right eye abducts) AND crosses to the LEFT MLF, which activates Left CN III (Left eye adducts).
